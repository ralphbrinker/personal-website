The Promise

People pay attention to your product because of a promise. You told them to expect more speed, productivity, or better status. You guarantee that all they need to do is to join you.

The promise is the only thing in their mind when they use your product for the first time.

They don't care about all the fancy settings and possible integrations. They won't see the value of all those timed animations in your onboarding flow. Or even check the sample projects you carefully put together.

All that matters is the promise.

What needs to happen, so people understand your product and experience the value proposition? Think about where you should land these people first, so they get it, and the fastest route to it.
